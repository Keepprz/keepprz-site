01 — No percentage, ever

We get paid for the work, not a cut of your business.

One flat monthly fee, one flat fee per booking. Your rate is locked for a full year — so a great season is yours to keep, not a bigger bill. No setup fee. No surprises. No percentage, no matter how much you earn.

02 — You own your guests

Airbnb doesn't give you customers. It lends them to you — and can take them back anytime.

Airbnb hides your guests' contact info, routes every message through the app, and can suspend you without warning — taking your reviews and your income with it. A direct booking site is yours. Every guest's email and phone number is yours. That's how you earn repeat stays, referrals, and a business you actually own.
